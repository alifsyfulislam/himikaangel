Who We Are

We were established as a Hemicas Angels in 2019 and began to operate internationally in 2020. From the beginning, we have been passionate about making a difference in people’s lives through our youth empowerment presentations, our humanitarian Builds program.

Our Mission

Hemica's Angels for Humanity’s mission is to combat Hunger, and provide sustainable healthcare and educational resources for underprivileged women and children in Bangladesh.

Our Vision

A country where women and children do not go to bed hungry and have access to healthy diet, proper healthcare and education in order to facilitate their growth and development.

Hemica's Angels for Humanity provides nutritious meals, health services, clean water, and educational opportunities to impoverished communities. Watch the videos and read below to learn more about how this program is transforming disadvantaged women and children's populations in Bangladesh
